Frequently Asked Questions

What AI models power MicroAI Hub? ▼
MicroAI Hub is powered by a combination of the latest large language models, fine-tuned specifically for content creation tasks. Our proprietary optimization layer ensures outputs are always high-quality, unique, and ready to use.
Do I own the content AI generates for me? ▼
Yes, 100%. Any content generated using your credits is entirely yours. You can use it, sell it, modify it, or publish it under your own name. We claim no rights over your generated content.
How does the credit system work? ▼
Each AI generation uses a certain number of credits depending on the tool and output length. Credits are replenished monthly based on your plan, or you can purchase additional credit packs at any time. Credits never expire on paid plans.
How do payouts work for sellers? ▼
When a buyer purchases your service, the payment is held for 48 hours (buyer protection), then released to your wallet. You can withdraw to PayPal, Stripe, or crypto at any time with a minimum of $20.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Absolutely! Our Starter plan is free forever with 50 credits/month. For Pro features, we offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Cancel anytime during the trial with no charges.
